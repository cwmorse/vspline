About vSpline

What is vSpline?

vSpline is a 3D modeling application that allows users to intuitively create, manipulate, analyze, and 3D print physical prototypes all within virtual reality. Traditional 3D modeling tools are clunky with a steep learning curve. Through the power of immersion and 3D input, users can now express their ideas through a tool unlike anything before.
